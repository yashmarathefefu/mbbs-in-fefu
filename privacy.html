Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FEFU Medical Intake collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us through forms, including:

  • Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Inquiry details regarding MBBS courses at FEFU.
  • Preferred country of origin for academic counseling.

2. Technical & Analytics Data

To improve your experience, we silently collect technical data through local storage and analysis tools:

  • IP Address and approximate geographic location (City/Country).
  • Device information (Browser type, Screen resolution, OS).
  • Browsing behavior (Time spent on pages, scroll depth, and interaction heatmaps).
  • Visitor Identification (via first-party cookies/storage) to recognize returning visitors.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide personalized academic counseling.
  • Contact you via Phone or WhatsApp regarding your inquiry.
  • Analyze website traffic to improve our user experience.
  • Prevent fraudulent or duplicate submissions.

4. Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to ensure your data is stored securely. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to third parties.

5. Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy and the collection of technical analytics data for the purpose of improving our service.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our administrative office through the official contact form on the homepage.
